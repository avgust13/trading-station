Ticker Glossary
  • SPY — SPDR S&P 500 ETF. Tracks the S&P 500 — the 500 largest US companies. Broad large-cap benchmark.
  • QQQ — Invesco QQQ Trust. Tracks the Nasdaq-100 — 100 largest non-financial Nasdaq names. Tech-heavy growth proxy.
  • IWM — iShares Russell 2000 ETF. Tracks the Russell 2000 — small-cap US stocks. Classic risk-on / risk-off tell.
  • SMH — VanEck Semiconductor ETF. 25 largest semiconductor companies. Key AI / cyclical-growth barometer.
  • XBI — SPDR S&P Biotech ETF. Equal-weighted biotech basket. High-beta speculative health gauge.
  • DIA — SPDR Dow Jones Industrial Avg ETF. Tracks the Dow 30 — 30 US blue-chip industrials/financials.
  • XLF — Financial Select Sector SPDR. S&P 500 financials — banks, insurers, asset managers, exchanges.
  • TLT — iShares 20+ Year Treasury Bond ETF. Long-dated US Treasuries. Moves inverse to long-end yields.
  • XLE — Energy Select Sector SPDR. S&P 500 energy — oil & gas majors, refiners, services.
  • XLK — Technology Select Sector SPDR. S&P 500 tech — software, hardware, semis.
  • CL1! — WTI Crude Oil — front-month future. West Texas Intermediate crude futures, front month. Global energy macro tell.
  • BTC — Bitcoin (USD spot). Bitcoin price in US dollars.
  • ETH — Ethereum (USD spot). Ethereum price in US dollars.
